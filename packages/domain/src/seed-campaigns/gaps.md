# National seed campaign gaps ()

This document records intentional geographic and thematic gaps in the  quality-first
national seed. **Quality determines count** — gaps are documented rather than filled with weak
or unverified records.

## Geographic gaps

| Gap | Reason |
| --- | --- |
| **Northeast depth** | Only Cheyney University (PA) and sparse Dunbar (DC) anchor the Northeast; Maine, Vermont, New Hampshire, Rhode Island, Connecticut, New Jersey, and New York lack seed records pending NRHP/NPS-verified candidates. |
| **Mountain West / Pacific Northwest** | Rosenwald sample includes one Arizona school; Montana, Idaho, Wyoming, Colorado, Utah, Nevada, Washington, and Oregon have no seed records — few Rosenwald or HBCU listings exist there. |
| **Alaska and Hawaii** | No HBCU or Rosenwald inventory; no verified candidates met the evidence bar. |
| **Upper Midwest** | HBCU sample uses Missouri; Wisconsin, Minnesota, Iowa, and the Dakotas lack Black educational institution seeds beyond future NRHP work. |
| **Locality-precision desegregation sites** | Scott's Branch (SC) and Mansfield (TX) ship at `locality` tier where campus coordinates are not source-documented at exact-site precision. |

## Thematic gaps

| Gap | Reason |
| --- | --- |
| **Rosenwald bulk corpus** | Fisk Rosenwald Fund Card File remains `deferred-unverified` in  launch corpora; Rosenwald campaign uses only NRHP/NPS individually verified listings. |
| **Full HBCU inventory** | NCES lists ~100 HBCUs; seed ships 5-sample plus overlapping Freedmen's/Movement entries — not a bulk import. |
| **Freedmen's Bureau school inventory** | Thousands of Bureau schools existed; only institutions with federal NPS/ED founding documentation ship. |
| **Desegregation litigation exhaustiveness** | Sample covers Brown companions and post-Brown crises, not every district court order or state case. |
| **Freedom School summer 1964 map** | Mississippi Freedom Schools are represented via Tougaloo anchor only; individual church-hosted sites await NRHP verification. |
| **Private K-12 historically Black academies** | Campaign focuses on documented public, NRHP, or federal-list institutions; many private academies lack cleared bulk corpus paths. |

## Coverage summary (campaign v1.0.0)

At curation time the bundle targets **all four Census regions** with **28 total records** across
six campaigns — far below the `NATIONAL_SEED_MAX_RECORDS` cap of 50 and orders of magnitude
below any U.S. school inventory.

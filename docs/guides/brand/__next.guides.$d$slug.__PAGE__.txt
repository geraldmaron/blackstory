2:Tf0d,<h1 id="brand-language"><a class="heading-anchor" href="#brand-language">Brand language</a></h1>
<p><strong>Product name:</strong> BlackStory.</p>
<p><strong>Core line:</strong> History, pinned to place.</p>
<p><strong>Support:</strong> People. Places. Evidence. Context.</p>
<p>Copy is specific over sweeping, evidence before assertion, pride without
spectacle. Invite; do not lecture. Never sensational framings or completeness
overclaims.</p>
<p>The binding brand masters live in the repository <code>brand/</code> directory. Served
copies use role-based paths under <code>apps/web/public/brand/</code> (<code>lockup-*.png</code>,
<code>symbol-*.png</code>, open-graph assets, favicons). Call sites use those role names
or identity helpers, never product-prefixed filenames reconstructed by hand.</p>

<p>Copper is a navigational signal (about 10 to 15 percent of a composition):
active locations, selected filters, primary actions, evidence markers. Raw
Copper Pin never carries body-size text on light canvas. Dark theme is
first-class.</p>
<p>Flat matte fills only. No bevels, shadows, glows, or gradients.</p>
<h2 id="type"><a class="heading-anchor" href="#type">Type</a></h2>
<ul>

<li><strong>Inter</strong>: UI / body</li>
<li><strong>Source Serif 4</strong>: editorial / longform</li>

<p>All open-source. No licensed fonts.</p>
<h2 id="shape"><a class="heading-anchor" href="#shape">Shape</a></h2>
<p>Radii 8 / 16 / 28px (sm / md / lg). Flat matte fills. No ornamental motion.</p>
<h2 id="the-mark"><a class="heading-anchor" href="#the-mark">The mark</a></h2>
<p>Official lockup and book-and-pin symbol are artwork. Never reconstruct either by
typing the wordmark beside a bare symbol render.</p>
<h2 id="imagery-and-people"><a class="heading-anchor" href="#imagery-and-people">Imagery and people</a></h2>
<p>Place first. Evidence visible. When people appear, they are identified (person /
role / place / year), never anonymous decoration. Avoid generic "Black history"
stock imagery, automatic sepia filters, AI images presented as documentary
material, and maps without source or precision context.</p>
<h2 id="map-dignity"><a class="heading-anchor" href="#map-dignity">Map dignity</a></h2>
<p>No red or alarm hues for violence-adjacent records. No crime-heat rendering.
Color is never the only signal; confidence stays glyph-encoded. Points render no
sharper than stored precision. A coarsened point is never labeled as an exact
address.</p>

2:T547,<h1 id="research-methodology"><a class="heading-anchor" href="#research-methodology">Research methodology</a></h1>
<p>BlackStory pins history to place. A publishable record needs claims, evidence,
provenance, and confidence — not assertion alone.</p>
<h2 id="principles"><a class="heading-anchor" href="#principles">Principles</a></h2>
<ol>
<li><strong>Evidence before assertion.</strong> Public prose cites released evidence.</li>
<li><strong>Place is first-class.</strong> Locations carry precision; coarsened points are
never labeled as exact addresses.</li>
<li><strong>Confidence is visible.</strong> Uncertainty is glyph-encoded; color is never the
only signal.</li>
<li><strong>Living-person care.</strong> Unknown living status is treated as living; no public
residential addresses.</li>
<li><strong>Promotion gate.</strong> Research and LLM enrichment cannot publish. Human and
policy gates promote released projections only.</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="pipeline-high-level"><a class="heading-anchor" href="#pipeline-high-level">Pipeline (high level)</a></h2>
<p>Discovery adapters produce candidates → quarantine / triage → research cases →
claims and evidence → confidence lineage → promotion → public projection.</p>
